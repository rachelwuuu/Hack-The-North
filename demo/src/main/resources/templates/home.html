Proper Squat Form:

  1. Stand with feet a little wider than hip width with toes facing forwards.
  2. Drive your hips back, bend your knees and ankles, and press your knees slightly outwards.
  3. Sit into a squat position while still keeping your heels and toes on the ground, chest up and shoulders back.
  4. Aim to reach parallel -- knees are bent to a 90-degree angle, and keep them behind your toes.
  5. Press into your heels and straighten legs to return to a standing upright position.
